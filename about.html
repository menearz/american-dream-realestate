About the firm

Built on what built this country.

American Dream Real Estate LLC is a 100% veteran-owned, Texas-based real estate investment company. We buy single-family and small multifamily properties — often off-market and in any condition — to hold as long-term rentals. We are not a brokerage. We do not represent buyers or sellers in transactions. We are the buyer.

Veteran-Owned Business

Verified by the Texas Veterans Commission.

American Dream Real Estate LLC is 100% owned and operated by an American veteran who served in the United States military with an Honorable Discharge. The business is officially verified as a Veteran-Owned Business by the Texas Veterans Commission.

We bring military discipline, honor, and follow-through to every transaction. When we say we'll close on a date, we close on that date.
