About the firm

Built on what built this country.

American Dream Real Estate is a veteran-owned and operated, Texas-based brokerage founded on the belief that owning your own home — a foundation for you and your family to build on, grow in, and pursue happiness from — is still possible for the American people.

We work with first-time buyers, veterans, and investors who are pursuing the American Dream. Our office is in Houston. Our listings are across the greater Gulf of America — Humble, Kingwood, Atascocita, The Woodlands, Spring, Cypress, and the neighborhoods in between.

We were founded by people who bought their first homes the slow way — by saving, by asking the right questions, by trusting the right broker. We try to be that broker for the next family.

Our listings are written carefully. Our advice is given honestly. Our paperwork is filed when we say it will be filed.

"Strength and honor."
