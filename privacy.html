Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how American Dream Real Estate LLC ("American Dream Real Estate," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information about visitors and users of the website at the-american-dream.net (the "Site") and the related communications channels operated by the company.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you contact us through the Site or related channels, including:

  • Contact information: name, email address, phone number.
  • Property information: address, condition, and circumstances of any property you share with us in your inquiry.
  • Communication content: the messages and information you submit in our contact form or send by email, SMS, WhatsApp, or phone.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and communicate with you about your property or partnership interest.
  • Evaluate properties you have asked us to consider purchasing.
  • Provide our real estate investment services to you.
  • Maintain internal records of inquiries and transactions.

3. Communications Consent (SMS, WhatsApp, Email, Phone)

By submitting your contact information through this Site or by contacting us through any channel, you expressly consent to receive communications from American Dream Real Estate LLC by SMS text message, WhatsApp message, email, and phone call related to your inquiry and the real estate services we provide. This includes follow-up messages, property questions, offer details, and scheduling.

Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies based on your inquiry. Your consent is not required as a condition of any purchase.

4. Opt-Out

You may stop receiving communications from us at any time:

  • SMS / WhatsApp: Reply STOP to any text message to unsubscribe from SMS or WhatsApp messages from us.
  • Email: Reply to any email asking us to remove you, or use the unsubscribe link when present.
  • Phone: Tell us verbally to stop calling, and we will note your request and stop.
  • You may also email us at americandreamtx93@gmail.com with a request to stop all communications.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate the Site and our communications:

  • Cloudflare — hosting, DNS, and content delivery for the Site.
  • Resend — transactional email delivery for contact form submissions.
  • Twilio — SMS, WhatsApp, and voice communications platform used to message and call inquirers.

These providers process information on our behalf under their own privacy and security terms. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

6. Data Retention

We retain contact information and inquiry records for up to 24 months after your last interaction with us, unless you request earlier deletion. Records connected to a completed real estate transaction are retained for longer periods as required by Texas property law and tax recordkeeping rules.

7. Your Rights

You may request to:

  • Access the information we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate information.
  • Delete your information from our records.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at americandreamtx93@gmail.com.

8. Children's Privacy

The Site is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the Site after a change takes effect constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

10. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy can be sent to americandreamtx93@gmail.com or by phone at (346) 423-6549.
