Demo notes

Slowly waving flag, just like the wallpaper.

13 stripes fill the hero. The canton with 50 stars sits anchored upper-left. Diagonal shadow ripples drift across to suggest fabric folds catching the wind. The whole flag billows on a 14-second cycle, hinged at the left like a flagpole. Pure CSS — no images, no JavaScript — and it pauses gracefully for users with prefers-reduced-motion set.

To use it on any page, give a <section> the class ad-flag-hero, and include the four child layers: .ad-flag-hero__flag, .ad-flag-hero__canton (with the 50-star SVG inside), .ad-flag-hero__ripple, and .ad-flag-hero__vignette, then your content wrapper .ad-flag-hero__content.

Watch the flag wave. Wait for the ripples to roll across.
