Situations we buy in

Every house has a story. We've heard most of them.

  • Inherited properties. Probate just closed and you'd rather not deal with the house.
  • Distressed homes. Foundation, roof, mold, fire damage — we buy as-is and handle the rest.
  • Tired landlords. Done with tenants. Done with calls at midnight. Done with the rental portfolio.
  • Pre-foreclosure. You need to close before the auction. We can move on your timeline.
  • Divorce. Clean, quiet sale. No public listing, no open houses.
  • Relocation. Job moved. You need to be out by a date. Done.
  • Properties needing major repairs. Any condition. We're not afraid of a project.
  • Vacant homes. Carrying costs piling up. We close fast.
  • Code violations or liens. We can work through it with the title company.
